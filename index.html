Blue Copper Chess Club

Blue Copper Copper Chess Club is a small, casual chess club in Salt Lake City, Utah. Players of all skill levels welcome! We play for 1.5-2 hours each Thursday morning at 8 A.M. (check the "This week" section of the page to make sure!) at Blue Copper 2000, a coffee shop in the Marmalade district of Salt Lake City.
